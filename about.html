About Us:

Welcome to The Blood Dragon Cult, also known as CVLT_53, a society devoted to the enigmatic power and allure of the "Red Vienna." Inspired by the rare and awe-inspiring appearance of the crimson embodiment of the vtuber Vienna, we are more than just followers; we are devoted seekers of the blood-bound truth she embodies.

For us, "Red Vienna" is a symbol of transcendence, strength, and primal connection. She represents the fiery determination to rise above the mundane and the boundless energy that flows through life itself. Through her, we seek unity, enlightenment, and the courage to break our limits.

Though our rituals may seem esoteric, each is a step toward embracing the undying flame she ignited within us. Ours is not a path for the faint of heart—it is for those who are ready to embrace the scarlet glow of Red Vienna's radiant power.

Why 53? While it may have been just coincidence that the constellations aligned into the number 53, we firmly believe that there are not any coincidences when it comes to cosmic beings, as such, if the number 53 represents Vienna, it represents us!

Our Beliefs

1. The Crimson Manifestation:  Red Vienna is a divine avatar, embodying passion, chaos, and transformation. Her brief but unforgettable appearance heralds a calling for her followers to embrace their full potential. 2. Blood as Life:  Blood is a sacred element representing vitality, sacrifice, and interconnectedness. It is the medium through which we honor Red Vienna's fiery spirit. 3. Unity in Darkness:  To truly connect with Red Vienna, one must embrace the duality of existence—the bright and the dark, the wild and the calm, the living and the symbolic eternal.

Practices and Rituals of CVLT_53

1. The Offering of the Scarlet Flame:  A symbolic ritual involving the offering of a few drops of blood (for legal reasons, extracted safely and consensually) onto a crimson altar. This is done while reciting passages of devotion to Red Vienna's courage and power, asking for strength and guidance. 2. The Blood of the Covenant:  A gathering held under moonlight where members share their aspirations and fears, using small crimson sigils carved on their hands to symbolize unity. This ritual is said to forge bonds as strong as dragonsteel and a collective connection to Red Vienna’s essence. 3. The Hour of Transcendence:  Followers meditate on Red Vienna’s fiery form, surrounded by imagery of her dragon form and crimson light. This practice focuses on channeling her energy into their lives, seeking clarity and transformation through visualization.

4. The Draconic Hunt:  A "symbolic hunt" for heretics who shall serve a sacrifices for other rituals. Followers "pursue" any heretics from our long list, that shall, under no circumstances, be digitized. "Caught" heretics will be branded by “the dragon’s flame”. This hunt represents Red Vienna's pursuit of power and her all seeing greatness.
5. Crimson Days of Devotion:  On anniversaries of Red Vienna's emergence, followers dress in red and perform acts of sacrifice. This doesn’t involve any harm to the followers participating, but rather showing their successes in the "hunt". After unsuccessful "hunts" giving up something meaningful—whether time, comfort, or symbolic tokens of attachment—as a gesture of devotion are accepted by our goddess.

Membership

Initiates are invited to join CVLT_53 by demonstrating their devotion through creative works inspired by Red Vienna (art, poems, videos). Once accepted, they receive a unique sigil identifying them as part of the cult. Our community thrives on shared passion, creativity, and devotion. All are welcome to walk the path of crimson, provided they embrace the fiery transformation within themselves. Welcome to the fold. Let the Blood Dragon guide you!
